About Meghalaya Dev Community

We are an open community for developers, UI/UX designers, Tech Enthusiasts and anyone who is interested in Technology.

Why Join Us?

  • Get updates with information about open source development in Meghalaya.
  • If you have an idea or any open source project share them here.
  • Discover the best tools and practices
  • Meet developers from Meghalaya
  • Grow your network

What is Dev.Connect Event?

  • A Meetup where developers, UI/UX designers, Tech Enthusiasts and anyone who is interested in IT can connect with each other.
  • A Platform to showcase side-projects, share ideas, share knowledge and their interests.
  • To listen to technical talks by resource persons
  • To have a good time and enjoy LIVE Music together
  • It's OPEN to all
